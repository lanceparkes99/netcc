About Us

Welcome to the official web site of the NorthEast Tarrant Coin Club. 17 years ago, this club was started by Merle Owens and Russell Prinzinger with the purpose of providing community-minded coin collectors a place to meet and share the hobby of coin collecting. The club has grown to over 100 members from all over North Texas.

Please join us every 2nd Thursday of the month at 7PM at Martin United Methodist Church, 2621 Bedford Rd, Bedford, TX 76021.

Meeting Schedule - Every 2nd Thursday of the Month, 7PM

Meeting Location - Martin United Methodist Church, 2621 Bedford Rd, Bedford, TX 76021

Meeting Activities:

  • Fellowship
  • Auctions
  • Raffles and Door Prizes
  • Educational Presentations
  • Opportunities to Learn and Serve
